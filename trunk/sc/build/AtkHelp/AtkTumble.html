AtkTumble Ambisonic Tool Kit B-format Tumble


AtkTumble.kr(w, x, y, z, angle)


Tumble an ambisonic B-format sound field clockwise 'upwards' the y-axis.


w, x, y, z - the B-format signals. 

angle - the angle of rotation in radians, from -pi to pi. pi/2 rotates what was front center to up and -pi/2 rotates to down. Both pi and -pi will rotate what was front center to back center. The default, 0, results in no change.



{ 

#w, x, y, z = AtkMonoToB.ar(PinkNoise.ar * LFSaw.kr(-10).range(0, 0.2), -0.5pi);

#w, x, y, z = AtkTumble.ar(w, x, y, z, MouseX.kr(-pi, pi));

AtkPantoF.ar(4, w, x, y, 1, -1);

}.play(s);
