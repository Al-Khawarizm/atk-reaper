AtkAtoB Ambisonic B-format transform


AtkAtoB.ar(a, b, c, d, orientation, mode)


Transform input A-format soundfield to B-format soundfield. Note: a coincident A-format input is expected; inputs are not equalized to generate coincidence from spaced microphone inputs.


a, b, c, d - input A-format signal

orientation - orientation of channels of resulting A-format soundfield, as follows:

0 - flu - front left up: FLU, FRD, BLD, BRU

1 - fld - front left down: FLD, FRU, BLU, BRD

2 - flr - front left-right: FL, FR, BU, BD

3 - fud - front up-down: FU, FD, BL, BR

4 - fbd - front and back down: F, BD, BLU, BRU

5 - fbu - front and back up: F, BU, BLD, BRD

6 - flru - front left-right up: FLU, FRU, FD, B

7- flrd - front left-right down: FLD, FRD, FU, B


mode -Â  W scaling mode, as follows:

0 - can - canonical: 1/sqrt(2)

1 - dec - decorrelated: 1/sqrt(3)

2 - uns - unscaled: 1


Output channels are in order W,X,Y,Z.
