RttB Ambisonic B-format imager


RttB.ar(w, x, y, z, rotate, tilt, tumble)


Rotate input B-format soundfield around z-, x-, and y-axis. 


w, x, y, z - input B-format signal

rotate - rotate left (from x-axis to y-axis) around the z-axis, in radians

tilt -  rotate up (from y-axis to z-axis) around the x-axis, in radians

tumble - rotate up (from x-axis to z-axis) around the y-axis, in radians


Output channels are in order W,X,Y,Z.
